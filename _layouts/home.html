About me


    I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the College of William & Mary, fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yifan Sun.
    Previously, I received my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Florida and my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Beijing Technology and Business University.
    My research broadly centers on computer architecture. I am particularly interested in exploring wafer-scale GPU architecture and developing tools for computer architecture simulation. I am still learning, and always eager to deepen my understanding through both research and collaboration.
